Validating Web Forms

First Letter of your First name must be capitalize and contain 3-16 characters

First Letter of your Last name must be capitalize and contain 3-16 characters

Email must be a valid address, e.g example@gmail.com

Password must be alphanumeric (@, _ and - are also allowed) and between 6-20 characters

A valid Mobile number (10 digits)

Bio must contain lowercase letters, underscores, hypens and be 8-50 characters
